Have you ever wondered what is the average capacity of a stadium in the top football divisions in European countries? Probably not, because hardly anyone cares about it. In fact, it doesn't even seem to be entertainment for grounhoppers or groundspotters. The purpose of this program, however, is to present the possibilities that Python offers in the area of web scraping, data processing and visualisation.

The source of the data is the Polish website http://stadiony.net. There are a lot of interesting facts on the site, but you won't find any information about the average capacity of a stadium for a given league. However, it will be calculated by the program built into this page. The data is taken dynamically, so it may happen that the next time you click the submit button, the results may be different. Although major changes can be expected next season, when the composition of the top leagues will be slightly different.
